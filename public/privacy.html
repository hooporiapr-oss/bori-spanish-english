Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

Who We Are

Hey Bori is a product of GoStar Digital LLC, a company based in San Juan, Puerto Rico. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your information.

What We Collect

Hey Bori is designed to be simple and private. We do not require you to create an account to use the free tier. When you use Hey Bori, your conversation messages are sent to our server to generate responses. We do not store your conversations on our servers after the session ends.

We may collect basic, anonymous usage data such as page visits and message counts to improve the service. We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it.

How We Use Your Information

Any information we collect is used solely to provide and improve the Hey Bori service. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies and Local Storage

Hey Bori uses your browser's local storage to track your daily message count for the free tier. This data stays on your device and is not sent to our servers.

Third-Party Services

Hey Bori uses Anthropic's Claude API to power conversations. Messages you send are processed through Anthropic's API to generate responses. Please refer to Anthropic's privacy policy for information on how they handle data.

Children's Privacy

Hey Bori is a general language learning tool. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Your Rights

Since we do not store your conversations or require accounts for the free tier, there is minimal personal data to manage. If you have any questions about your data, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact GoStar Digital LLC at San Juan, Puerto Rico.
